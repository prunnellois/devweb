Our restaurant

We wanted our restaurant to be just like us: a warm space where friends and family could meet. We also wanted it to be a place of design and closeness to nature, because we want to look to the future with these two disciplines.

Our favorite moment of the day

Because of its beautiful colors and warmth, sunset is our favorite time of day. If we were at the water's edge, we would offer you the chance to eat our dishes on the beach, but if that's not possible, come and enjoy them in our patio.
